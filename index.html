Sources & honest limits

§1 Messaging = CleverTap campaign dashboard (source of truth; the event-export mirror undercounts campaign sends/clicks). §2–4 behaviour/timing/app = event-stream export, ~5-day retention, directional. §5 tickets = service-ticket warehouse table (49 days).
